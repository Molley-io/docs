# CRM

Molley's built-in CRM helps you manage companies, contacts, and opportunities in one place.

## Overview

The CRM helps you:

- **Track companies** you're targeting or working with
- **Manage contacts** and relationships
- **Follow opportunities** through your pipeline
- **Organize outreach** and follow-ups

## CRM Structure

### Companies

Organizations you're tracking:

- Prospects you're targeting
- Customers you're serving
- Partners you're working with

### Contacts

People within companies:

- Decision makers
- Champions
- Users
- Influencers

### Opportunities

Potential deals or engagements:

- Sales opportunities
- Partnership discussions
- Expansion deals

## Companies View

### Creating Companies

1. Navigate to **Growth** → **CRM**
2. Click **+ New Company**
3. Enter company details:
   - Name
   - Website
   - Industry
   - Size
   - Notes

### Company Details

Each company card shows:

- **Company name** and logo
- **Industry** and size
- **Contacts** count
- **Opportunities** count
- **Last activity** date

### Expanding Companies

Click a company to see:

- Full company details
- Associated contacts
- Related opportunities
- Activity history

## Contacts

### Adding Contacts

1. Open a company
2. Click **+ Add Contact**
3. Enter contact details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Role/Title
   - Phone
   - Notes

### Contact Information

Track for each contact:

- **Name** and title
- **Email** and phone
- **Role** in buying process
- **Notes** and history

## Opportunities

### Creating Opportunities

1. Click **+ New Opportunity**
2. Enter opportunity details:
   - Name
   - Company
   - Value
   - Stage
   - Expected close date

### Pipeline Stages

Default stages:

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Lead** | Initial interest |
| **Qualified** | Confirmed fit |
| **Proposal** | Sent proposal |
| **Negotiation** | Discussing terms |
| **Won** | Deal closed |
| **Lost** | Did not close |

### Pipeline View

Switch to Pipeline view to see:

- Kanban board of opportunities
- Drag and drop between stages
- Total pipeline value
- Stage-by-stage breakdown

## Views

### Companies View

Default view showing:

- All companies as cards
- Expandable to show contacts and opportunities
- Search and filter

### Pipeline View

Kanban view showing:

- Opportunities by stage
- Drag to update stage
- Pipeline value summary

### Switching Views

Use the toggle at the top:

- **Companies** — Organization-centric view
- **Pipeline** — Deal-centric view

## Search and Filter

### Search

Find records quickly:

- Search by company name
- Search by contact name
- Search by opportunity name

### Filters

Filter the view:

- By industry
- By company size
- By opportunity stage
- By date range

## Best Practices

### 1. Keep Data Current

Accurate data enables good decisions:

- Update after every interaction
- Log important conversations
- Track stage changes promptly

### 2. Add Context

Notes make data useful:

- Why is this company a good fit?
- What are their specific needs?
- Who are the key stakeholders?

### 3. Regular Pipeline Review

Review opportunities weekly:

- What's progressing?
- What's stalled?
- What needs attention?

### 4. Connect to Outreach

Link CRM to your campaigns:

- Track which campaigns generated leads
- Measure conversion by source
- Optimize based on results

### 5. Use Tags

Organize with tags:

- Industry segments
- Lead source
- Priority level
- Campaign association
