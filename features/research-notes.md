### New Note

1. Navigate to **Research** â†’ **Research Notes**
2. Click **+ New Page** in the sidebar
3. Enter a title for your note

### Nested Pages

Create a hierarchy by nesting pages:

1. Hover over an existing page in the sidebar
2. Click the **+** icon that appears
3. A new child page is created
4. Drag and drop to reorganize
