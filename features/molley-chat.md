## Tips for Better Conversations

### Be Specific

Instead of: "Help me with marketing"

Try: "Write 3 email subject lines for our Q1 product launch targeting enterprise buyers"

### Reference Your Data

Instead of: "What should our roadmap look like?"

Try: "Based on our feature ideas and customer feedback, what should we prioritize next quarter?"

### Use Follow-ups

Molley remembers the conversation. Build on previous responses:

1. "Analyze our competitor positioning"
2. "Now suggest how we can differentiate"
3. "Turn that into a one-liner for our homepage"

### Add Context When Needed

If you're discussing something specific, load it first:

1. Navigate to the relevant document
2. Click the **Context** button to load it into chat
3. Then ask your question
