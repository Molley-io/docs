# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Molley in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit [molley.io](https://molley.io) and click **Sign Up**
2. Create your account using email or social login
3. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Create Your Company

After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first company:

1. Enter your **Company Name**
2. Add a brief **Description** of what your company does
3. Optionally upload a **Logo**
4. Click **Create Company**

::: tip
You can create multiple companies later if you work with different organizations or clients.
:::

## Step 3: Create Your First Project

With your company set up, create a project:

1. Click **+ New Project** in the sidebar
2. Enter your **Project Name** (e.g., "Mobile App", "SaaS Platform")
3. Add a **Description** explaining what this product does
4. Define your **Target Audience**
5. Click **Create Project**

## Step 4: Explore the Interface

Once your project is created, you'll see the main navigation:

### Left Sidebar
- **Company selector** — Switch between companies
- **Research** — Notes, knowledge base, data synthesis
- **Product** — Wiki, features, Kanban, bugs, feedback
- **Growth** — Outreach, marketing, CRM

### Top Header
- **Help** — Access guided tours and documentation
- **Molley Chat** — Open the AI assistant
- **User Menu** — Profile settings, dark mode, browser extension

## Step 5: Try Molley AI

Click **Molley Chat** in the header to open the AI assistant. Try asking:

- "What should I focus on first for my product?"
- "Help me write a product description"
- "What features should I consider for my target audience?"

The AI already knows about your company and project — no setup required!

## Step 6: Install the Browser Extension (Optional)

The browser extension lets you capture research from any webpage:

1. Click **User Menu** → **Browser Extension**
2. Download for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
3. Pin the extension to your toolbar
4. Click the Molley icon on any page to save content
