# Creating Your First Company

A Company in Molley is your workspace — it contains all your projects, team members, and settings.

## Company Overview

When you create a company, you're setting up:

- **A shared workspace** for your team
- **Company-wide context** that the AI uses to assist you
- **Billing and subscription** management
- **Member permissions** and access control

## Creating a Company

### Basic Information

1. Click your **Company name** in the sidebar
2. Select **Create New Company** or navigate to company settings
3. Fill in the required fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Your company or organization name |
| **Description** | Brief overview of what your company does |
| **Logo** | Optional brand image |

### Company Details

For better AI assistance, complete your company profile:

#### Company Tab
- **Industry** — Your business sector
- **Size** — Number of employees
- **Website** — Your company URL
- **Founded** — When the company started

#### Profile Tab
- **Mission Statement** — Your company's purpose
- **Vision** — Where you're heading
- **Values** — Core principles that guide decisions

#### Business Tab
- **Business Model** — How you make money
- **Target Market** — Who you serve
- **Competitive Advantage** — What sets you apart

#### Culture Tab
- **Work Style** — Remote, hybrid, in-office
- **Communication Style** — Formal, casual, etc.
- **Team Structure** — How teams are organized

::: tip Why Complete Your Profile?
The more information you provide, the better Molley AI can assist you. When you ask for help with marketing copy, product decisions, or strategy, the AI uses this context to give relevant, on-brand suggestions.
:::

## Managing Team Members

### Inviting Members

1. Go to **Company Settings** → **Members**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Admin** — Full access, can manage billing and members
   - **Member** — Can create and edit content
   - **Viewer** — Read-only access

### Member Permissions

| Permission | Admin | Member | Viewer |
|------------|-------|--------|--------|
| View all content | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Create/edit content | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Manage projects | ✓ | ✓ | ✗ |
| Invite members | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Manage billing | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |
| Delete company | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ |

## Billing & Subscription

### Subscription Types

Molley offers flexible pricing:

- **Solo Mode** — For individual users with token-based AI credits
- **Team Subscription** — Seat-based pricing for organizations

### Managing Billing

1. Go to **Company Settings** → **Billing**
2. View your current plan and usage
3. Upgrade, downgrade, or manage payment methods

## Switching Between Companies

If you belong to multiple companies:

1. Click the **Company name** in the sidebar
2. Select from your available companies
3. The interface updates to show that company's projects and data
