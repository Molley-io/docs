# Browser Extension

The Molley browser extension lets you capture research, save content, and interact with Molley AI from any webpage.

## Why Use the Extension?

When researching competitors, reading articles, or browsing the web, you often find valuable information. The browser extension lets you:

- **Save content** directly to your research notes
- **Capture screenshots** of important pages
- **Highlight text** and save it with context
- **Quick-add ideas** without leaving your browser
- **Access Molley AI** for instant assistance

## Installation

### Chrome / Edge / Brave

1. Open Molley and click **User Menu** → **Browser Extension**
2. Click **Download for Chrome**
3. The Chrome Web Store opens
4. Click **Add to Chrome**
5. Confirm the installation
6. Pin the extension to your toolbar for easy access

### Firefox

1. Open Molley and click **User Menu** → **Browser Extension**
2. Click **Download for Firefox**
3. The Firefox Add-ons page opens
4. Click **Add to Firefox**
5. Confirm the permissions
6. The Molley icon appears in your toolbar

## Getting Started

### Signing In

1. Click the **Molley icon** in your browser toolbar
2. Click **Sign In**
3. You'll be redirected to authenticate
4. Once signed in, the extension connects to your account

### Selecting Context

Before capturing content, select where it should go:

1. Click the Molley icon
2. Choose your **Company**
3. Choose your **Project** (optional)
4. Content will be saved to this context

## Capturing Content

### Save Full Page

1. Navigate to a page you want to save
2. Click the Molley icon
3. Click **Save Page**
4. The page URL, title, and content are saved to your research

### Highlight and Save

1. Select text on any webpage
2. Right-click and choose **Save to Molley**
3. Or click the Molley icon and click **Save Selection**
4. The highlighted text is saved with the source URL

### Screenshot Capture

1. Click the Molley icon
2. Click **Capture Screenshot**
3. Select the area to capture (or full page)
4. Add notes if desired
5. The screenshot is saved to your research

### Quick Ideas

Found something that sparks a feature idea?

1. Click the Molley icon
2. Click **Add Idea**
3. Enter a title and brief description
4. The idea is added to your Feature Ideas backlog

## Using AI from the Extension

The extension includes quick access to Molley AI:

1. Click the Molley icon
2. Click **Ask Molley**
3. Type your question
4. Get AI assistance with context from the current page

### Example Uses

- "Summarize this article"
- "How does this compare to our product?"
- "What features are they highlighting?"
- "Extract the key points from this page"

## Settings

### Configure Defaults

1. Click the Molley icon
2. Click the **gear icon**
3. Set your preferences:
   - Default company and project
   - Keyboard shortcuts
   - Notification preferences

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
|--------|----------|
| Open extension | `Alt + M` |
| Save selection | `Alt + S` |
| Quick idea | `Alt + I` |
| Screenshot | `Alt + C` |

::: tip Customize Shortcuts
You can customize these in your browser's extension settings.
:::

## Privacy & Permissions

### What the Extension Accesses

- **Active tab** — To read content you want to save
- **Storage** — To save your preferences locally
- **Cookies** — To maintain your login session

### What We Don't Do

- We don't track your browsing history
- We don't access tabs you haven't interacted with
- We don't sell or share your data

## Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Working

1. Make sure you're signed in
2. Check that the extension is enabled
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Restart your browser if needed

### Content Not Saving

1. Verify you've selected a company/project
2. Check your internet connection
3. Ensure you have permission to save to that project

### Can't Sign In

1. Clear the extension's data
2. Sign out of Molley in your browser
3. Sign back in to both
